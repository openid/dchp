# Digital Credentials Harmonized Presentation (DCHP)

This is the working area for the specifications of the OpenID Foundation's
[Digital Credentials Harmonized Presentation (DCHP) Working Group](https://openid.net/wg/digital-credentials-harmonized-presentation-working-group/).

## Background

The DCHP Working Group supports a joint initiative between experts of
ISO/IEC JTC 1/SC 17 (WG10) and the OpenID Foundation's
[Digital Credentials Protocols (DCP) Working Group](https://openid.net/wg/digital-credentials-protocols/)
to harmonize their credential presentation protocols.

[ISO/IEC 18013-5](https://www.iso.org/standard/69084.html) (Device Request / Device Response) and
[OpenID for Verifiable Presentations](https://openid.net/specs/openid-4-verifiable-presentations-1_0.html)
(Authorization Request / Authorization Response) take different approaches to
credential presentation. The working group is developing a technical
specification for a harmonized digital credentials request protocol that
brings these together, supporting both in-person and online presentation
and the coexistence of multiple credential formats, including ISO mdoc and
IETF SD-JWT VC.

See the [working group home page](https://openid.net/wg/digital-credentials-harmonized-presentation-working-group/)
for more information.

## Contributing

See the [guidelines for contributions](CONTRIBUTING.md).
The working group operates according to its
[Working Procedures](https://openid.net/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/DCHP-WG-Exhibit.pdf).
In particular, note that an OpenID Foundation
[IPR Contribution Agreement](https://openid.net/intellectual-property/)
covering the DCHP Working Group is required before contributing.

## Meetings

The working group meets regularly by conference call. The schedule and
joining details are published on the
[working group home page](https://openid.net/wg/digital-credentials-harmonized-presentation-working-group/).

## Who do I talk to?

- Questions and proposals about the specifications themselves are best raised
  in the [issue tracker](https://github.com/openid/dchp/issues).
- Join the [mailing list](https://lists.openid.net/mailman/listinfo/openid-specs-dchp)
  to follow the working group's announcements and discussions
  (note that only people with an active IPR Contribution Agreement can post
  to the list).
- The co-chairs can be contacted directly at
  [openid-specs-dchp-owner@lists.openid.net](mailto:openid-specs-dchp-owner@lists.openid.net).
